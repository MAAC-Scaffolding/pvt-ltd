MAAC SCAFFOLDING PRIVATE LIMITED

Establishment

  • MAAC Scaffolding PRIVATE LIMITED established in 2018
  • Started business in 2006 as Trading & Manufacturing under the names ‘Mehta Trading’ & ‘Mehta Industries’
  • Combined in 2018 as “MAAC Scaffolding Private Limited”
  • Became a manufacturer & supplier of scaffolding & formwork systems

Introduction

MAAC Scaffolding is a reputable company specializing in Aluminium formwork accessories. With a strong presence in the construction and scaffolding industry, we offer a wide range of high-quality products and services.

Core Business: Manufacturing and supplying aluminium formwork accessories and all scaffolding items for the construction industry.

Offerings

Formwork Accessories: We offer a comprehensive range of formwork accessories, including wall ties, wedge pins, scaffolding brackets, and other Mivan accessories.

Custom Solutions: MAAC Scaffolding can tailor solutions to meet specific project requirements, ensuring versatility and adaptability.

Quality Assurance: Committed to maintaining the highest quality standards, our products are rigorously tested and certified to meet industry benchmarks.

Nationwide Reach: MAAC Scaffolding has a widespread distribution network, serving clients not only locally but also on nationwide scale.
